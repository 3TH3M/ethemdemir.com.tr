ZINK VTOL

Autonomous Cargo VTOL System for Emergency Response

Emergency Response & Aviation 2023
5kg Payload Capacity
45 min Flight Time
40km Operating Range

Project Overview

The ZINK VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) system was developed as a response to the growing need for rapid emergency equipment transport in open waters. This autonomous system combines advanced UAV technology with emergency response capabilities to deliver critical supplies to areas that are difficult to access by traditional means.

Key Features

Fail-Safe System

Advanced autonomous flight control system with comprehensive fail-safe mechanisms for reliable operation.

Payload Management

Sophisticated payload system capable of carrying and delivering up to 5kg of emergency supplies.

Real-time Telemetry

Live video feed and telemetry data transmission for continuous mission monitoring.

All-Weather Operation

Weather-resistant design enabling operations in various environmental conditions.

Technical Specifications

Flight Performance

  • Maximum Flight Time: 45 minutes
  • Range: 40km

  • Payload Capacity: 5kg
