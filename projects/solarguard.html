Project Overview

SolarGuard is an innovative autonomous drone system designed for efficient solar panel inspection and maintenance. By combining thermal imaging and computer vision technologies, it provides comprehensive analysis of solar panel health and performance, helping to optimize energy production and reduce maintenance costs.

Key Features

Thermal Analysis

Advanced thermal imaging for detecting hot spots and potential failures in solar panels.

Autonomous Inspection

AI-powered autonomous flight system for systematic panel inspection.

Performance Analytics

Comprehensive data analysis and reporting of panel performance metrics.

Maintenance Planning

Automated maintenance recommendations based on inspection data.

Technical Specifications

Drone Specifications

  • Flight Time: 40 minutes
  • Maximum Range: 10km
  • Payload Capacity: 2kg
  • Operating Altitude: 5-50m

Sensors & Cameras

  • High-resolution Thermal Camera
  • 4K RGB Camera
  • LIDAR for Obstacle Avoidance
  • GPS & IMU Integration

AI Capabilities
